I'm Richard

Hi, thanks for checking out SponsorPair. If we've not met before I'm the hackathon evangelist at Devpost. As part of my role I've spoken to 1000's of hackathon organizers and the companies that sponsor them. Two of the questions I'm often asked are "Can you help us find sponsors?" and "Which hackathons should I sponsor?". Making introductions that enable great events and sponsors to connect is always great.

I created SponsorPair to do just that - at scale.

SponsorPair doesn't take any fee to connect events and companies. I'm simply looking to make connections.

Events submitted will the categorized into an email sent to companies that have opted-in to receive notifications about technology community events.
